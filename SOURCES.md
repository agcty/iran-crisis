## Methodology Notes

- Oil prices use settlement/close prices except where noted as intraday
- Dubai physical prices reflect Platts assessments, which can differ from futures
- EU gas storage interpolated linearly between AGSI/Kyos data points
- VIX values for non-trading days (weekends) are interpolated
- Gold trajectory verified to show the counterintuitive decline, not the initially assumed safe-haven rally
- Jet fuel prices interpolated between confirmed Platts assessments on specific dates
- All 31-day arrays cover Feb 28 (Day 1) through Mar 30 (Day 31), 2026
