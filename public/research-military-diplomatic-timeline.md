## I. OPERATION EPIC FURY / ROARING LION (US-Israel)

### Feb 27 -- Authorization
- **20:38 UTC**: President Trump gives the order to proceed with Operation Epic Fury.

### Feb 28 -- Day 1: Strikes Begin
- **06:35 UTC**: CENTCOM announces it "and partner forces" have begun airstrikes against Iran. Tomahawk missiles, HIMARS, B-2 stealth bombers deployed.
- **06:45 UTC**: Israeli Air Force launches Operation Roaring Lion -- decapitation strikes. Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei killed at his residential compound alongside family members (daughter, granddaughter, son-in-law, daughter-in-law). ~200 Israeli fighter jets involved; largest combat sortie in Israeli history.
- **Multiple cities hit**: Qom, Kermanshah, Isfahan, Karaj, Tehran.
- Trump announced strikes via 8-minute Truth Social video at 2:00 AM EST. Stated objective: "eliminate imminent threats from the Iranian regime."
- Netanyahu: Israel acted to remove "the existential threat" to the state. "Justice has been served."
- Defense Minister Israel Katz: "Justice has been served, axis of evil suffered mortal blow."
- Sources: [NPR](https://www.npr.org/2026/02/28/nx-s1-5730352/world-leaders-reaction-operation-epic-fury), [USNI News](https://news.usni.org/2026/02/28/u-s-israel-launch-operation-epic-fury-against-iran-tehran-retaliates-across-region), [Army Recognition](https://www.armyrecognition.com/news/army-news/2026/us-israel-launch-operation-epic-fury-against-iran-nuclear-program-and-missile-arsenal), [Wikipedia - 2026 Iran war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Iran_war)

### Feb 28 -- Iran Retaliates
- **09:05 UTC**: Iran launches ~170 ballistic missiles toward Israel and Gulf states.
- Strikes hit: Al Udeid Air Base (Qatar), Ali Al Salem Air Base (Kuwait), Al Dhafra Air Base (UAE), Fifth Fleet HQ (Bahrain), targets in Israel (Haifa, Tel Aviv -- 1 woman killed, 27 injured).
- IRGC transmitted VHF warnings: "No ships would be permitted to pass" the Strait of Hormuz. Tanker traffic dropped ~70%; 150+ ships anchored outside the strait.
- Sources: [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/israel-iran-attack-02-28-26-hnk-intl), [Wikipedia - Timeline](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_the_2026_Iran_war)

### Mar 1 -- Day 2
- B-2 bombers target hardened ballistic missile facilities. Tehran Revolutionary Court destroyed. Intelligence officials killed.
- IRGC claims striking USS Abraham Lincoln (denied by US).
- Missile breaches Israeli defenses: synagogue/homes in Beit Shemesh hit -- 9 killed, 65 injured.
- 6 US service members killed (announced by CENTCOM Mar 2).
- **Khamenei's death confirmed** by Iranian government. Celebrations and mourning simultaneously across Iran.
- Ali Larijani announces interim leadership council.
- UK PM Starmer approves US use of British bases for "defensive" operations.
- Source: [Wikipedia - Timeline](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_the_2026_Iran_war), [NPR](https://www.npr.org/2026/02/28/1123499337/iran-israel-ayatollah-ali-khamenei-killed)

### Mar 2 -- Day 3
- IRGC officially confirms Strait closure. Ebrahim Jabari, senior IRGC adviser: "The strait is closed. If anyone tries to pass, the heroes of the Revolutionary Guard and the regular navy will set those ships ablaze."
- Qatar: Two Iranian Su-24 bombers shot down (first manned Iranian aircraft lost).
- Three US F-15Es crash in Kuwait -- CENTCOM attributes to friendly fire.
- Trump: US "knocking the crap out of Iran" with more attacks coming.
- Source: [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/3/2/iran-says-will-attack-any-ship-trying-to-pass-through-strait-of-hormuz)

### Mar 3 -- Day 4

- CENTCOM Adm. Brad Cooper: "destroyed 17 Iranian ships" including IRIS Fateh submarine (first sub sunk since Falklands War).
- Israeli F-35I "Adir" shoots down Yak-130 over Tehran (first stealth fighter downing manned jet in combat).
- Golestan Palace (UNESCO site) damaged -- UNESCO issues statement on violation of international law.
- Iranian Red Crescent death toll: 787.
- **Mojtaba Khamenei elected new Supreme Leader** by Assembly of Experts (Mar 8 formally).
- Source: [Wikipedia - Timeline](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_the_2026_Iran_war)

### Mar 3 -- Trump on Navy Escorts

## III. TRUMP ADMINISTRATION -- KEY STATEMENTS & ACTIONS

### Mar 9 -- Trump expresses intent to seize strait control.

### Mar 11 -- SPR Release
- Trump authorizes release of **172 million barrels** from Strategic Petroleum Reserve. Part of coordinated IEA release of **400 million barrels** globally. Delivery over ~120 days.
- Energy Secretary Chris Wright: US arranged to "more than replace these strategic reserves with approximately 200 million barrels within the next year -- 20% more barrels than would be drawn down -- and at no cost to the taxpayer."
- Sources: [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/11/iran-war-trump-oil-strategic-petroleum-reserve.html), [DOE](https://www.energy.gov/articles/united-states-release-172-million-barrels-oil-strategic-petroleum-reserve), [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/03/12/oil-prices-reserve-iran-war)

### Mar 19-20 -- Iran Sanctions Waiver
- Treasury Secretary Bessent: US issues 30-day sanctions waiver for sale of Iranian oil at sea (~140 million barrels).
- Bessent: "In essence, we will be using the Iranian barrels against Tehran to keep the price down as we continue Operation Epic Fury."
- On where the oil goes: "It can flow into Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, Japan, India [which] have been good actors in this."
- Bessent ruled out government intervention in oil futures markets; focus on "boosting physical crude availability."
- Critics: FDD called it "funding the enemy" -- oil worth >$14 billion for Tehran at current prices.
- Sources: [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/us-issues-30-day-sanctions-waiver-for-sale-of-iranian-oil-at-sea.html), [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2026/03/20/politics/iran-oil-sanctions-lifting), [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/03/19/trump-iran-oil-sanctions)

- Trump on Truth Social at 23:44 GMT: "If Iran doesn't FULLY OPEN, WITHOUT THREAT, the Strait of Hormuz, within 48 HOURS from this exact point in time, the United States of America will hit and obliterate their various POWER PLANTS, STARTING WITH THE BIGGEST ONE FIRST."
- Source: [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/3/22/trump-issues-48-hour-hormuz-strait-ultimatum-threatens-iran-power-plants), [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/03/22/trump-iran-48-hour-ultimatum-strait-of-hormuz), [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/newsletters/2026-03-23/trump-gives-iran-48-hour-ultimatum-to-reopen-strait-of-hormuz)

## IX. IRAQ

### Oil Export Collapse
- Southern exports halted after Iranian strikes on tankers/infrastructure. Basra terminals shut.
- Output plunged: **4.3M bpd to 1.4M bpd**, exports near zero.

### Kirkuk-Ceyhan Pipeline Restart
- Iraq worked to rehabilitate Kirkuk-Ceyhan pipeline to Turkey's Mediterranean port.
- Oil Minister Hayan Abdul Ghani: ~100km of testing remains; potential 200-250K bpd.
- **~Mar 18**: Iraq resumed exports via Kirkuk-Ceyhan after deal with Kurdistan Regional Government. ~250K bpd pumped to Ceyhan.
- Sources: [The National](https://www.thenationalnews.com/business/energy/2026/03/16/iraq-works-to-revive-kirkuk-ceyhan-pipeline-as-southern-exports-halt/), [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-10/iraq-oil-output-cut-further-as-baghdad-pushes-for-kirkuk-restart), [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/economy/2026/3/27/saudi-uae-iraq-can-three-pipelines-help-oil-escape-strait-of-hormuz)

## XV. OPEC+ RESPONSE

### Mar 1 -- Emergency Meeting
- 8 members met (Saudi Arabia, Russia, UAE, Kazakhstan, Kuwait, Iraq, Algeria, Oman).
- Agreed to raise production by **206,000 bpd** (debated range: 137K-548K bpd) for April.
